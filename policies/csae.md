# Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE) Policy

## 1. Purpose
We are committed to protecting children and preventing the use of our platform for any form of child sexual abuse or exploitation. This policy outlines our standards, prohibitions, enforcement actions, and reporting mechanisms related to CSAE.

## 2. Zero-Tolerance Policy
We maintain a **zero-tolerance policy** toward child sexual abuse and exploitation. Any violation of this policy will result in immediate action, which may include account suspension or termination and reporting to appropriate authorities.

## 3. Prohibited Content and Behavior
The following are strictly prohibited on our platform:

- Child sexual abuse material (CSAM), including any visual, audio, written, or simulated content
- Sexual content involving minors, regardless of intent or perceived consent
- Grooming behaviors, including attempting to establish a sexual or exploitative relationship with a minor
- Sexual solicitation of minors
- Sexualization or fetishization of minors in any form
- Requests for, distribution of, or facilitation of CSAM
- Instructions, encouragement, or normalization of CSAE-related activities
- Role-play or fictional scenarios involving sexual content with minors

This prohibition applies whether the content is explicit, implicit, real, simulated, or fictional.

## 4. Detection and Moderation
We take reasonable and appropriate measures to detect, prevent, and remove CSAE-related content, which may include:
- Automated content analysis
- User reporting mechanisms
- Manual review and moderation
- Enforcement of platform safeguards and restrictions

## 5. Reporting and Cooperation with Authorities
When we become aware of CSAE or CSAM:
- We act swiftly to remove the content and restrict involved accounts
- We report confirmed CSAM to relevant authorities, including the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC) or equivalent organizations, as required by law
- We cooperate fully with law enforcement investigations

## 7. Enforcement Actions
Violations of this policy may result in:
- Immediate content removal
- Temporary or permanent account suspension

We reserve the right to take any action necessary to protect minors and comply with legal obligations.

## 8. Alignment with External Standards
Our CSAE standards are informed by and aligned with established child safety frameworks and best practices, including:
- Online child protection and safety-by-design principles
- Industry-recognized child safety and trust standards
- Applicable local and international laws and regulations
