Celo

Celo is a modern messaging and calling application designed to help people communicate easily, securely, and meaningfully.

The app allows users to send messages, share photos and media, send voice notes, and make audio and video calls with friends, family, and new connections.

Celo focuses on simplicity, privacy, and high-quality communication without unnecessary clutter. Our goal is to make conversations feel natural, personal, and human.
