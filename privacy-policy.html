Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2026

Celo respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the Celo messaging and calling application.

Information We Collect

  • Phone number and account identifiers (for authentication)
  • Profile information such as name and profile photo
  • Messages, media, and voice notes you choose to send
  • Call-related data necessary to provide audio and video calls
  • Device and usage information for performance, security, and diagnostics

Permissions

Celo may request the following permissions:

  • Camera: to capture profile photos and share images or video
  • Microphone: to record voice messages and make calls
  • Storage: to send and receive media
  • Notifications: to alert you of new messages and calls

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide messaging and calling features
  • To improve app performance and reliability
  • To maintain security and prevent abuse

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Information is only shared with trusted service providers such as Firebase, which help us operate core app functionality.

Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information. However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Children’s Privacy

Celo is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your Rights

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your personal information by contacting us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of Celo means you accept the updated policy.
